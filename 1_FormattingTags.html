HARRY POTTER - THE MAGICAL WORLD

Introduction

Harry Potter is a globally renowned fantasy book series written by British author J.K. Rowling The series follows the life and adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, along with his best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Set in the magical world,the story revolves around Harry’s journey from being an orphan to becoming the savior of the wizarding world by defeating the dark wizard, Lord Voldemort. The Harry Potter series consists of seven books, each covering a year of Harry’s life at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

Characters

The Harry Potter series is filled with a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique personalities and backgrounds. Some of the main characters include: 

    Harry Potter1      :   The protagonist of the series, an orphaned wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his 11th birthday.
    Ron Weasley2       :   Harry’s best friend and a member of the Weasley family, known for his loyalty and sense of humor.
    Hermione Granger3  :   Another of Harry’s best friends, known for her intelligence and resourcefulness.
    Albus Dumbledore4  :   The headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, a wise and powerful wizard.

Themes

The Harry Potter series explores a wide range of themes, including:
Love and sacrifice
Friendship and loyalty
Good vs. evil
Identity and belonging
Death and mortality
